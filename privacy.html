Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how GenPlay (operated by Chongqing Pioneer Office Equipment Co., Ltd., developer: Tieguo Zhu) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Phone Number: We collect your phone number for registration and login purposes via SMS verification.
  • User Profile: We store your user ID, username (if provided), first login time, and synthesis statistics (such as synthesis count and last synthesis date).
  • Synthesis Records: We store the text you submit for text-to-speech (TTS) conversion, the resulting audio URL, synthesis status, and timestamps.
  • No Sensitive Data: We do not collect your email, address, location, device information, contacts, photos, or any other sensitive personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our core services, including user authentication and text-to-speech conversion.
  • To manage and track your synthesis quota and history.
  • To prevent abuse and ensure fair use of our services (e.g., rate limiting).
  • We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, or analytics purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

  • LeanCloud: We use LeanCloud for cloud data storage and SMS verification. Your phone number and synthesis records are securely stored on LeanCloud servers.
  • Tencent Cloud TTS: For text-to-speech functionality, the text you submit will be securely transmitted to Tencent Cloud TTS for audio synthesis. No personal identification information (such as your phone number) is sent to Tencent Cloud. For more information, please refer to Tencent Cloud Privacy Policy.
  • We do not share your personal data with any other third parties.

4. Data Security

  • Your data is transmitted over secure (HTTPS) connections and stored using industry-standard security practices.
  • We rely on LeanCloud and Tencent Cloud's security and compliance measures to protect your data.

5. Your Rights

  • You may access your synthesis records and quota information after logging in with your phone number.
  • If you wish to delete or correct your personal data, please contact us at the email address below. We will process your request as soon as possible.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete such data.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
